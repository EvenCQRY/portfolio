Master's degree in physics

I took my master's degree of physics in École Normale Supérieure de Lyon, where I chose to shift my focus towards theoretical physics. You can find below a list of the courses I took. Hover for more details.

First year

  • General Relativity

    An introductory course to general relativity. Essential material covered:

    • Elementary differential geometry
    • Einstein field equations
    • Schwarzschild metric
    • Applications to stars and cosmology
  • Quantum Field Theory

    An introductory course to quantum field theory. Essential material covered:

    • Poincaré group study
    • Classic scalar field
    • Quantization of the scalar field
    • Coupling with an external source
    • S-matrix, probability of scattering events
  • Astrophysics

    Essential material covered:

    • Celestial mechanics
    • Stars physics
    • Astrophysical disks
    • Introduction to cosmology (FLRW, different expansion scenarios...)
